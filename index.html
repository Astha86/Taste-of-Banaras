Baati Chokha

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Ghee
  • Salt
  • Vegetables

Recipe

  1. To make baati, mix wheat flour, ghee, salt, and water for stiff dough.
  2. Shape dough into balls and bake until golden brown and cooked through in oven or tandoor.
  3. For Chokha, peel off roast brinjals and then mash with onions, tomatoes, chilies, salts, coriander. Serve with baati, dal, pickle, and ghee.

Chaat

Ingredients

  • Boiled Potatoes
  • Papdi
  • Chutneys
  • Sev

Recipe

  1. Peel off potatoes and Cut them into small cubes and set aside.
  2. Heat butter, add cumin seeds. Saute onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, salt, spices, tomatoes, potatoes and cook until they turn soft. Garnish with papdi and sev, a squeeze of lemon juice, and sprinkle some chaat masala.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khowa
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • All-purpose flour, baking powder

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, flour, baking powder for smooth dough.
  2. Roll dough into balls, fry in ghee until golden. Cook on low heat to prevent burning.
  3. Make sugar syrup with equal parts sugar and water. Add flavorings. Remove from heat, cool slightly. Soak fried gulab jamuns in syrup for 30 minutes.
